Attribute
- An attribute is a quality or feature that someone or something has.
- Usually one of a number of characteristics, such as size or shape or color

Feature
- A feature of something is an interesting or important part or characteristic
of it.

Characteristic
- A *distinguishing* quality or prominent feature
- The ~s of a person or thing are the qualities or features that *belong to*
them and make them *recognizable*.

Property
- The properties of a substance or object are the ways in which it behaves
in *particular conditions*, such as color, weight, height, length, etc.

Trait
- A trait is a *particular* characteristic, quality, or tendency that someone
or something has.
