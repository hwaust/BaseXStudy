What is the difference between suggestion and advice?
2015-12-31

A suggestion is more a proposal-an idea.
Advice is more considered and based on the facts of a given situation and
experience 

If I give you a suggestion-it's something you might like to think about
If I give you advice, then that is something I think you should think about


Suggestion
An idea or proposal put forward for consideration. A psychological process by
which an idea is induced in or adopted by another without argument, command.

Advice
Recommendation about action: somebody's opinion about what another person
should do Opinion about what could or should be done about a situation
or problem; counsel.
